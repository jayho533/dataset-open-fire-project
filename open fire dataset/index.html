CAUSES OF FIRES IN NYC

Electrical Failures: Overloaded outlets, faulty wiring, and failing electrical systems in older buildings are driving a sharp rise in multi-alarm structural fires. Heating Equipment: Space heaters and old boilers spike in usage during winter months, causing fires when placed too close to curtains, furniture, or bedding

Careless Smoking: Discarded cigarettes igniting nearby trash, cardboard, or bedding lead to devastating and sometimes fatal building fires. Debris and Brush Burning: The largest cause of spring wildfires in the state. Landowners illegally burning yard waste or failing to follow seasonal regulations often see fires quickly spread to nearby timber.
